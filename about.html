About Us

The team behind GVB.

Global Vision Biotech (GVB) is a strategic market access and business development company helping international manufacturers of laboratory, life science, and medical technology enter and grow across the Middle East and Africa. Since 2014, GVB has helped manufacturers build the teams, partnerships, and commercial operations that turn market opportunity into lasting business.

Our leadership brings together decades of hands-on experience across laboratory technology, diagnostics, medical devices, and life sciences, backed by a board of advisors spanning commercial strategy, scientific research, healthcare innovation, and regional market expertise.
