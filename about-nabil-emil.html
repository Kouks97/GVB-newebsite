Board of Advisors

Nabil Emil

Strategic Advisor, Healthcare Technology & MedTech Leadership

Nabil Emil is a healthcare technology executive with more than 35 years of senior management experience across the Middle East and Africa. He is recognized for connecting advanced medical technologies with clinical, operational, and strategic priorities across government healthcare systems, private healthcare organizations, and technology providers.

From 2002 to 2024, Nabil served as General Manager of Gulf & World Traders, part of the Belhoul Group in Dubai, where he led a major business transformation that positioned the company as one of the UAE's leading medical technology providers. He developed a factory-trained engineering organization, achieved ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 certifications, and established strategic partnerships with more than 40 global MedTech manufacturers. Earlier in his career, as General Manager of SEPEI Egypt, he led operations for a major medical equipment provider, introducing advanced healthcare technologies that strengthened clinical capabilities nationwide.

Nabil has been a strong advocate for healthcare digital transformation, supporting the adoption of AI-enabled radiology platforms, remote monitoring solutions, tele-radiography, predictive equipment analytics, and digital screening programs. Through SEPEImed, he advises healthcare organizations on implementing scalable, regulatory-compliant healthcare solutions, including centralized sterilization services, national screening programs, AI-supported diagnostics, modular hospital solutions, and turnkey healthcare projects.

Previously, Nabil served as Chairman of the Dubai MedTech Business Group under the Dubai Chamber, contributing to policy development, innovation initiatives, and healthcare sector growth. Today, he continues to provide strategic advisory support to healthcare and MedTech organizations across the Middle East and Africa.

His experience combines deep regional market knowledge, operational execution, regulatory awareness, and practical expertise in transforming healthcare technology opportunities into sustainable business outcomes.
