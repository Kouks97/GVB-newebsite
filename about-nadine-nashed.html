Nadine Nashed

Creative Lead, Brand & Digital Experience

Nadine Nashed is a multidisciplinary product and experience designer specializing in brand strategy, digital experiences, and user-centered design.

With a background spanning luxury beauty, retail innovation, sustainability, and digital platforms, she combines creative thinking with strategic problem-solving to build experiences that connect brands, technology, and users.

During her experience with L'Oréal, Nadine contributed to retail innovation, customer experience development, and sustainability initiatives, combining design thinking with operational improvement. Her expertise includes UX strategy, digital design, prototyping, design systems, and visual storytelling.

At Global Vision Biotech, Nadine leads brand and digital experience, shaping how GVB communicates its vision, capabilities, and market value. She supports brand positioning, market research, digital strategy, and the development of GVB's digital presence across content, structure, and design.

Nadine holds a Pre-Master's degree in Product and Industrial Design from the German University in Cairo and has completed the Design Thinking and Innovation program at Harvard Business School.
